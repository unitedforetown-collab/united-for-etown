Our Candidates

Meet the community leaders we support for local office.

Corty Byron

Borough Council — Ward 2

Corty Byron is a lifelong Elizabethtown resident whose family has a history of public service. A working musician, Corty knows the value of listening and finding harmony in differences. He is running to bring people together and keep Elizabethtown a place we’re proud to call home.

Kevin Karr

Judge of Elections

Kevin Karr trained in culinary school, worked in professional kitchens, and built a career in the service industry. A lifelong Scout, he now leads younger scouts by living the Scout Oath. Kevin brings fairness, steadiness, and leadership to his campaign for Judge of Elections.
