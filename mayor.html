Why I’m running

Jamie for Mayor 2025 is about doing the basics well and telling you the truth. I want E-Town to run on clear goals, tight budgets, and steady follow-through so schools, streets, and downtown actually improve. When something isn’t working, I’ll say it plainly, fix it with the team, and show you the results.

What you can count on

  • Plain-language updates you can read in two minutes.
  • Budget discipline. Schools, safety, and downtown first.
  • Faster small fixes; real timelines for big work.
  • Respect in the room, even when we disagree.
  • Decisions based on data and lived impact—not headlines.

Priorities

Strong Schools

Back safe facilities, clear funding info, and real support for students.

  • Safe Routes to School: crossings, traffic calming, lighting.
  • Regular joint sessions with the district on facility needs.
  • After-school and youth partners for mentoring and wellness.
School priorities →

Safer Streets

Make it safer to walk, bike, and drive—especially near schools and parks.

  • Paint-and-post traffic-calming pilots on problem blocks.
  • Crosswalk beacons and timed school-zone speed enforcement.
  • Small fixes fast: potholes, signage, line striping.
Safety plan →

Downtown & Small Business

Make it easier to open, improve, and stay open on Main Street.

  • One-stop permit help and plain-language checklists.
  • Pop-up and storefront activation program.
  • Facade micro-grants with local partners.

Open, Honest Government

Tell you what’s happening, what it costs, and when it’s done.

  • Two-minute updates after meetings in plain language.
  • Budgets and projects published with simple charts.
  • Service request tracker with response targets.
