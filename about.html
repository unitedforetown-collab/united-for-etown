Our Mission

Bring neighbors together to support strong schools, safer streets, and a healthy local economy in Elizabethtown. We work on practical steps that improve daily life and respect how people differ.

Quick facts

  • Focus: schools · safety · small business

Our Values

  • Transparent communication
  • Data-informed decisions
  • Fiscal responsibility
  • Service over headlines

FAQ

Is United for E-Town partisan?

No. We’re a local coalition focused on practical fixes that help families and small businesses.

How can I get involved?

Start with a small step: sign up to canvass a block, share a post, or help at an event.

Where does my donation go?

Outreach materials, community events, and tools that help neighbors connect and take action.

Do you meet in person?

Yes. Check the Events page for upcoming gatherings and town-hall style conversations.
