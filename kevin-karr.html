Kevin Karr

Candidate for Judge of Elections

About Kevin

Kevin Karr trained at culinary school, worked in professional kitchens, and built a career in the service industry. He knows the importance of precision, timing, and fairness — skills he brings to public service and election administration.

A lifelong Scout, Kevin leads younger scouts by living the Scout Oath and modeling calm, steady leadership.

What the Judge of Elections Does

  • Oversees polling place operations on Election Day.
  • Works with inspectors and clerks to protect ballot integrity.
  • Ensures a fair, accessible process for every voter.
