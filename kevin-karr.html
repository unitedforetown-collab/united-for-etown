Kevin Karr

Judge of Elections Ward 1

About Kevin

Kevin Karr trained at culinary school and worked in professional kitchens for years. He brings precision, timing, and calm problem-solving to public service.

He’s a lifelong Scout and now a troop leader. He teaches the same steady, service-first approach he learned growing up.

Skills from the kitchen

  • Precision and clear record-keeping
  • Timing and queue management
  • Calm under pressure
  • Fair, consistent standards
  • Team leadership on busy days

Scout Oath he lives by

  • On my honor, I will do my best
  • To do my duty to God and my country and to obey the Scout Law
  • To help other people at all times
  • To keep myself physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight

What a Judge of Elections does

  • Runs the polling place on Election Day
  • Works with inspectors and clerks to protect ballot handling
  • Supports an accessible process for every voter
